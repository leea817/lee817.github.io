와인 (wine)

Fill me with anger;
Fill me with guilt;
Fill me with sorrow;
Fill me with solitude.

Fill me up with anything you’d like
but I beg of you do not fill me up with bliss;
      for bliss is nothing but a disguise
           for the ephemeral.

                Promise me
to fill me up like
bitter wine,
drink me up with
a fragile glass,
wipe my renmants with
a feeble cloth
and in return I will fill
you up with truth;



                the truth of loneliness.
